ABOUT ME

I first picked up the guitar 8 years ago. What inspired me then was the abundance of youtube acoustic covers. However, due to a lack in motivation to learn (and the pain of learning an instrument with zero guidance), I gave up on learning the guitar. 10 years ago, resources were very limited and the only way to learn was through someone you knew.

Fast forward 4 years later, I was in the army, and I had the money to buy a guitar, as well as the time to learn it again. And so, I began learning how to play the guitar again. I had friends who played the guitar before and guided me along the way, and after much practice and development of my caluses on my fingertips, I was able to play the guitar.

At this point in my life, I think I may actually spend some time to learn a little bit of music theory. At my current level, I still have to rely on an app (Ultimate Guitar) to play a song. With music theory however, you would be able to play a song just by listening to it figuring out the key of the song using the guitar. Hopefully, I will have the time and energy to spend time on that. :')
