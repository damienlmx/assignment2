Guitar Basics

Self-Taught, with 4 years of Experience

Hi! Im Damien.

I started learning how to play the guitar during my 2nd year of army, and I've been practicing ever since.

I had little exposure to music theory prior to picking up the guitar (and still have a very limited understanding of music theory), so the focus of the blog is not so much music theory, but more of the basics of playing a guitar.

In this blog, I will share more about myself and my journey in playing the guitar, as well as the basic chords that I started with.

I will also be sharing some songs that you can play with the chords taught in this blog.
